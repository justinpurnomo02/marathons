Welcome to My Running Journal

Hello! I’m Justin, a casual runner based in Berkeley, CA. I grew up in Los Angeles and moved to Berkeley to pursue a PhD in chemistry.

I started running during COVID in 2020 as a way to get out of the house and stay in shape. Since then, I’ve fallen in love with distance running. It has taken me to incredible places and taught me valuable lessons about perseverance, patience, and pushing beyond my limits.

This site is my running journal where I mainly share detailed race reports and reflections from each marathon. Whether you're a fellow runner looking for course insights or just curious about the marathon experience, I hope you find something inspiring here.
